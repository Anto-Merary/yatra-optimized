About Us

The Biggest Inter-College Cultural & Technical Festival of the Year.

YATRA is not just a fest; it's a movement. We bring together thousands of young minds from across the state to celebrate music, dance, fashion, and technology.

Experience two days of non-stop energy, celebrity performances, competitive events, and memories that last a lifetime. Whether you're a coder, a dancer, or a dreamer, Yatra is your stage.
